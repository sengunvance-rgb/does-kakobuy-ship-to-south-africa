### Q2: Will I have to pay customs duties and taxes in South Africa?
A2: Yes, almost certainly. Kakobuy's shipping costs do not include South African customs duties and VAT. When your parcel arrives, it will be assessed by SARS. If you use a courier service, they will typically contact you for payment before delivery. For economy shipping via SAPO, you'll be notified to collect and pay at the post office.
